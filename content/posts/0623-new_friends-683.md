I was just thinking that around this time last year, I moved away from some long time friendships that were no longer a positive influence on my life.  At the time, it was really hard to do. 
Looking back however, I realize that moving on enabled me to meet new people and I feel like this last year I have developed some great new friends.  So, friends, I'm happy to have you around, and I feel my life is possibly at it's most positive peak right now. Thanks everyone!
