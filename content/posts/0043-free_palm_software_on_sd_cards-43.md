[Mobile Digital Media (MDM)](http://www.gomdm.com) is giving away a one software title to the first 5000 people who provide them with a little personal info about themselves (name, address, phone number, that soft of stuff).  You even get to choose the software title you would like to receive.  The choices are:

- Rayman
- Atari Classic
- Sega Classic
- Dictionary/Thesaurus
- Travel Software

The software is available on SD cards, but if you have an SD card reader for your PC, you can transfer the apps to another media (like Memory Stick for us Clie users).
Oh, and the link for this offer is here: [http://www.gomdm.com/registration.asp](http://www.gomdm.com/registration.asp)
