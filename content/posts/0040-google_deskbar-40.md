![](http://toolbar.google.com/deskbar/images/deskbar.gif)
I installed the new [Google Deskbar](http://toolbar.google.com/deskbar/index.html) from [Google Labs](http://labs.google.com) yesterday, and I must say it's pretty nifty.  It adds a Google search to your taskbar making it easy to find things.  Plus, you don't have to open your browsers. It has it's own mini-browser built-in.  I use the Opera web browser and it also has it's own search box at the top that I have set to Google, but I like how easy the deskbar makes it to search using other searches than the normal Google.  One click of a button and you can access searches such as News, Groups, Images, Froogle.  You can also set custom searches.  It comes with a definition and thesaurus.  Plus they include a movie review search from [Rotten Tomatoes](http://www.rottentomatoes.com/).  Also a download search from [CNet's Download.com](http://download.com).  I added my own custom search from [Acronym Finder](http://www.acronymfinder.com), making it easy to figure out those strange acronyms. I'd say anyone who loves Google (and who doesn't), should try this out.  It's a free download, though still not a finished product.
